Luxon

A powerful and friendly wrapper for Javascript dates and times.

                
DateTime.local().timezone('America/New_York').minus({ weeks: 1}).endOf('day').toISO();

Features

  • DateTime, Duration, and Interval types.
  • Immutable, chainable, unambiguous API.
  • Parsing and formatting for common and custom formats.
  • Native timezone and Intl support (no locale or tz files).
